5. Theoretical lessons
Some basic principles of see navigation:
  • The see map and the most important topographical signs.
  • Contour lines –hydrograph.
  • Distance definition – estimated distance.
  • Cross bearing.
  • Double bearing
 
Additional equipments:
  • Buoys
  • Lift bags - function & usage & safety.
  • Reels &lines.
  • Compass.
 
Searching:
  • To note/mark the last known position and find it again.
    • Cross bearing.
    • Compass bearing.
    • Distance
  • To choose the correct searching profile:
  • Circle
  • Spiral (circular or rectangle).
  • UW - sample.
  • Lines.
  • After contour (deep) lines
  • Carrying out:
    • Sight & distance of the lost things, overlap.
    • Start & goal, to limit and divide the searching area.
    • Communication &marking.
  • Recovery
    • The selection and use of lifting bags.
    • Safety measures
