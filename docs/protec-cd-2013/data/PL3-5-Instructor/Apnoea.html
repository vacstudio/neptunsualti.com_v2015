Apnoea 2
Advanced Apnoea skills for Free diver training.
Apnoea 3
Excellent Apnoea skills up to competition training.
Professional Level Apnoea 1
The skills of Apnoea 1 (=Snorkel Diver) diver are already part of the Scuba Diver 1 course, so each Scuba Instructor may train and certify Apnoea 1 Diver.
The goal is to provide good snorkel skills to prepare best for the way to the Scuba courses like Scuba Diver 1. Included are equipment knowledge (mask, snorkel fins), proper swim, descent & ascent techniques, pressure & lungs, buoyancy and protection against cold.
