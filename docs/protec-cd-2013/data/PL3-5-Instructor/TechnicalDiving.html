Technical Diving
„Technical“
Career:
1.)     The instructor candidate must know the exact course process (and standards) of the ProTec course types. Repetition and recess of the course program which the candidate had already completed as ProTec Technical Diver is strongly recommended. Special Instructor know how (to identify ahead & solve possible problem of a course) is also part of the IDC.
2.)     As soon as the instructor candidate has acquired the necessary knowledge - this applies also to crossover candidates of other agencies – he concludes his qualification for the respective technical ProTec Instructor level with an exam (IE) taken by a ProTec Course Director or, if possible, the HQ replaces this by an internal exam of the qualification & references (see basics course).
3.)     All past or lower instructor certifications remain active. Career changers entering directly on a high level (e.g. Trimix) must fulfil all lower level conditions (Nitrox, Scuba AI). Therefore ID cards and certificates can be requested.
Nitrox 1 or 2 Instructor
(actually “Basis”Speciality)
Requirements: ProTec Scuba Instructor 1* plus Nitrox 1 or 2 Diver certificate
Completion:                ProTec Nitrox 1 or 2 Instructor Exam
Authorization:             Training and Certification of Nitrox Diver 1 / or 1 & 2
