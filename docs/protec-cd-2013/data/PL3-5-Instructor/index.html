ProTec INSTRUCTOR
An Scuba Instructor is someone, who is certified to train and examine students to take proper skills for Scuba diver levels.
Next, there are special levels for each Instructor status: Recreational Scuba and various Tec-levels (Technical diving).
Approximately, for each diver level a Instructor level exists.
The term “Professional Level” is starting with Dive Supervisor, the next level is Assistant Instructor, someone who successfully finished an IDC (Instructor Development Course). Then, after completing the IE (Instructor Examination/Evaluation), the first level authorised to certify divers, is ProTec Scuba 1* Instructor. The next ProTec Instructor level, ProTec Advanced Scuba 2** Instructor (AI), qualifies the Instructor to train and certify near all Scuba Diver Level, but not to train or certify any Tec-Diver Levels or any Instructor levels. (see ProTec authorisation concept). To become a Technical Diving Instructor, the candidate must be an AI and succeed the proper Tec-Instructor level examination.
