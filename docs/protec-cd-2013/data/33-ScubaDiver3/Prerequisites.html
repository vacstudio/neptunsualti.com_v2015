3. Prerequisites
A very good physical condition and fitness, very good diving skills and good skills in the specialities such as
  • Night Diving
  • Advanced Navigation
  • Search & Recovery
  • Deep Diving (40 msw)
  • OHE diving (cavern or any wreck/cave or ice diving)
  • Equipment Maintenance
  • Rescue Diver
  • Dive Leader
    all that should be proved through
    • appropriate speciality brevets
    • certification scuba 2** diver
    • confirmation of 60 logged dives
    • a medical statement.
    • minimum age is 18 years.
