Welcome as an ProTec - Instructor
We are pleased to welcome you as an active ProTec Instructor in our organisation. We work hard, in order to be considered as one of the best and most well- known training agencies within the range of the challenging, technical diving.
Our up to day success shows us that we are thereby on the correct way. Advertisement such as announcements, exhibitions, fairs etc., is internationally distributed. Naturally, such international activities have their price. For this the annual fees are a small amount. Stable price strategy, a good concept and dynamic co-workers are a key to our success.
Pro Tec is a trademark of a international enterprise consulting group, (Phoenix Consulting Ltd, KMJ Ltd & Partners), which as a sponsor supports our diving range. Primarily offering service enterprise with more than 20 years experience in management- consultation sector with big customers such as banks, insurance, as well as trade and industry. This can be also your advantage. We also offer consultation for diving schools, from marketing to the entire firm concept and we ensure also your professional Internet access in www.
For questions we are available –  E mail: contact@protecdive.com. All important information are reported in the Internet www.protecdive.com. For instructors under “members” there is an area which offers important, current, (and internal) information (Iog on with UserID=Instr.No.). This area is our information platform. All current standards, important information, course information, price lists, forms etc., are available there for reading or downloading, with which you keep current your ProTec instructor file.
We ask you, if you open your own diving center, to send us the filled-out center registration form, otherwise, your diving training place in your  registration will be named according to your past instructor training center.
We look forward to a good co-operation and wish much success.
With best regards
your ProTec Team
