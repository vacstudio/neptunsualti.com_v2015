1. Scuba Instructor Trainer (IT)
(PROTEC 3*** INSTRUCTOR)
An IT is skilled to train Instructor candidate students.
Active IT status qualifies and authorises for Instructor training done during an Instructor development course = IDC which is for first Instructor level - ProTec Scuba Instructor (I). IT status qualifies also for upgrade training to Instructor speciality levels like Night, Navigation, Deep, Rescue,..and so on till AI training.
The IT is also qualified to train and authorized to certify Dive Supervisor and Assistant Instructors Level.
