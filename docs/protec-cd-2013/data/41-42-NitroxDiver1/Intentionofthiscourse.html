1. Intention of this course
The Intention of this course is to arrange diving with standard Nitrox mixtures within 22% to 40% O2 through professional equipment and course so that the diver is able to use the advantages of Nitrox with regard to normal compressed air. This course is similar to most Nitrox “User” courses and ranks as one of the so-called special courses “Specialities”.
Instruction by ProTec Nitrox 1 instructor.
