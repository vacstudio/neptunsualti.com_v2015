6. Practical training
General dive skills checks
  • Diving time Apnoea 60 sec
  • Distance diving Apnoea about 45 meter
  • Apnoea deep diving 8 msw.
  • Scuba, about 5msw: Scuba gear UW off, take off the mask, Apnoea swimming - circle gear UW, take regulator again and gear on, then put on the mask
Classroom & Pool exercises:
  • To master the subject: Be able to draft any time by different chapters.
  • Be able to answer out - of - subject questions.
  • How to interact with complicated students & finical questions
  • To keep the topic and stay on the schedule.
  • To find the right style
  • Is everything under control? (Schedule, Students’ regulation, Safety)
UW exercises
  • Perform assistance for 3x SD 1 exercises - take care of dive students - watch out for any  problems
  • Perform assistance for 2x SD 2 exercises - take care of dive students - watch out for any  problems
  • Perform assistance for one SD 3 exercise - take care of dive students - watch out for any  problems
  • Perform 2x Rescue Diver skills / exercises
For open water environment: The instructor is not allowed to be incapable of acting (for example; through presenting an exercise). He should always be ready to intervene (to help the students). Set an Assistant to presentation if necessary.
