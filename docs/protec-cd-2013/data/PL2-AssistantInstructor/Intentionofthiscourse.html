1. Intention of this course
The Intention of this course is to prepare the advanced diver for the work as a scuba instructor and to offer all the necessary skills that are important to support the instructor as an assistant. Second stage of ProTec professional level = PL-2.
This could be done either by learning from scratch during long period work at a dive centre by supervision of an IT or during a intensive course, called IDC (Instructor Development Course).
Every candidate who successfully completed a IDC (but not a IE) may be also certified for Assistant Instructor.
Instruction by ProTec Scuba Instructor Trainer (3*** Instructor)
