3. Prerequisites
A very good physical condition and fitness likewise very good diving skills plus sufficient skill in the most important specialities such as night, navigation, current diving, boat diving and (simple) rescue diving  - that should be proved through
 
  • A medical statement
  • A certification of scuba 1* diver
  • 25 confirmation of TG practice.
  • The minimum age is 16 years.
