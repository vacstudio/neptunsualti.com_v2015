Video cave diving
ISBN 3-932470-21-4
Descrption:
An instructional film for cave diving courses for advanced sport divers. It contains all basic knowledge of this topic as well as techniques for advanced. UW- photographs of caves in Mexico, Florida and Dom. Rep.
The author, Martin Tolksdorf, is ProTec Cave Course Director & Instructor coach and trains cave divers from the beginner to the cave diving teacher.
Author: Tolksdorf, Martin, contributions by: Kremers, Gerd; Ritter, Dirk
Language: German
Length: approx. 50 min
