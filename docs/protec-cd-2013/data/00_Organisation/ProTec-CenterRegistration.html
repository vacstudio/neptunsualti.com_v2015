ProTec-Center Registration
Every ProTec instructor must be staff member of a registered ProTec dive centre to be able to certify students.
Registration for new ProTec dive centre is similar to any new diver registration.
Registration & certification online
Standard / recommended procedure is done online. There are two main steps.
  • The first step is the dive centre owner makes the data input. When finished, the owner will receive a temporary ID, which have to be passed to the Instructor for further processing.
  • Next step is for the Instructor. Go >centre certification< and type centre's temp. ID. Then certification panel with centre data is shown. Input certification = centre type code and ready done! If the centre owner is an ProTec Instructor he or she could do own centre certification otherwise any active ProTec Instructor may be asked for.
  • A detail information about Members Area registration & certification program is for download at Members Area.
