10. ProTec Progam Overview
ProTec offers course for technical as well as recreational divers.
  • Apnoea program
  • Basis program for beginners and advanced scuba diving
  • Mixed gas program with Nitrox as entry level for Rebreather and deep diving
  • OHE program with wreck and cave diving
Apnoea program:
Free diving without equipment has become very popular over time. However, it requires physical and mental training. The ProTec Apnoea course program offers an optimal training to the constantly growing number of free divers up to competition level.
Basic program:
The basic Scuba program offers newcomers the best start to sport diving with a first-class dive training and provide a high quality basement for technical diving. Main levels are Scuba Diver 1 up to Scuba Diver 3. Scuba specialities complete the Scuba program.
Mixed gas program:
The mixed gas program offers a safe alternative to “normal air” breathing with Nitrox course for sport divers. Nitrox is an excellent fundament for SCR & CCR and for extreme deep diving training program as well, like Extended Range, Trimix, Deep CCR.
As a technical diving agency we have a long and intensive experience for all kind of tec-stuff..
Wreck and cave diving:
The individual steps of the wreck and cave diving programs resemble themselves. There is a first introduction course followed by further training which should give you the possibility to dive into wrecks or caves at famous holiday locations.
For serious amateur explorers, we offer a program for advanced cave or wreck divers.
After some "Specials" and many years of practical experience we offer the possibility to prove your experience together with other professionals in a workshop to qualify as master of your diving speciality.
