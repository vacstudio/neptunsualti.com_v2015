Information about the certification procedure
Remember the course check list has to be completed - one copy goes to the graduate and one remain by the instructor.  Each copy has to be signed by the instructor and the graduate.
For an immediate certification please pay attention to the following guidelines.
Registration & certification online
Standard / recommended procedure is done online. There are two main steps.
  • The first step is the student (no matter if diver or instructor course candidate) data input. The Instructor should advise all students at the beginning of each course to input their data (at members area login panel) and upload a photo (identification card type) there. When finished, they will receive a temporary ID, which they should pass to their Instructor for further processing.
  • Next step is for the Instructor. Go >diver certification< and type student's temp. ID. Then certification panel with student data is shown. Input certification code, course data and ready done!
  • A detail information about Members Area registration & certification program is for download at Members Area.
Registration & certification via POST/MAIL not possible anymore!
If any problem concerning the registration form or the certification procedure arise - please contact us by email.
We are always pleased to help you.

Certification kit
The graduates should receive a certification kit to each certification which contains:
- Diver identification card
- Certificate
- A3 Folder
- A6 Folder
- ProTec sticker
- ProTec lanyard with slipcover for the card
- Welcome / information letter
- Manual (optional - if part of he course)
- Diving charts (optional - if part of he course)
- all together inside a ProTec course- bag
Trainer & Centres owner please ask your PCP or HQ about country specific material!
Caution! Never forget to hand over the completed and confirmed course check list!
