6. Practical Training
Exercises on land - first aid
  • Oxygen (explain and show different possibilities – there are many systems but less basic methods)
    • Open system
    • Circuit
  • Reactivation on hand of a dummy (doll) would be best
    • Artificial respiration
    • Cardiopulmonary resuscitation
    • Attend to injury

Exercises on the surface
  • Secure position and buoyancy
  • Transport of a beated diver to land/ship
  • Transport of a unconscious diver to land/ship
  • Artificial respiration methods
    • Without any tools
    • With snorkel
    • With breathing mask
  • Gear off / drop off parts of equipment
  • Diver on surface in panic or stress
    • Approaching method
    • Establish contact
    • Solve Problem

Exercises under water
  • Diver still conscious
    • Help for buoyancy (to much / to less)
    • Help against stress
    • Help against panic
  • Diver unconscious
    • Search und Find is part of the ProTec speciality course "Search &Recovery"
    • Start-up: Get the diver in upright position, ready for ascent
    • Ascending: Get the diver to the surface
      • Take care of position and hold
      • Take care of exhalation
      • Take care of buoyancy and ascending speed
